Get Started with LearnFlow

Follow these simple steps to download the app, create your profile, and start learning with your Agentic Assistant.

Note to Task Checkers / Evaluators

Please install and test the actual application on your device! We have worked extensively to ensure the UI looks premium, the interactions are smooth, and the Agentic AI works flawlessly. We highly encourage you to try the complete flow—from Onboarding (setting a goal) to asking the AI Tutor contextual questions within a Chapter.

We would be incredibly grateful for your time in testing it and for any feedback or ranking you can provide. It is incredibly helpful to our development!

1. Download the Application

Download the latest version of the LearnFlow app for your mobile device or tablet. The app is fully optimized for a smooth, offline-capable experience.

2. Create Your Profile

When you open the app, you will be guided through a quick onboarding screen. This is important! Make sure to enter your Class/Standard and your primary goal (e.g., "I want to be an Engineer" or "I love cricket"). The AI Tutor uses this profile to generate hyper-personalized analogies just for you!

3. Start Learning with AI

Navigate to any subject or chapter in your class. If you ever get stuck or need a simpler explanation, simply tap the floating "Learn with AI" button. The Agentic Tutor will instantly slide up, automatically knowing exactly which chapter you are reading!
